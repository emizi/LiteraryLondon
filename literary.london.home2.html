Mission Statement

Welcome to Sherlock's London, where we hope to guide you around some of the important places visited by Sherlock Holmes in various adaptations and by his creator Sir Arthur Conan Doyle.


The Sherlock Holmes museum is a treasure trove of memorabilia in Victorian style, to celebrate the great works of literature written by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle.
