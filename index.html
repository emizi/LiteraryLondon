About Sherlock's London

The Sherlock's London Tour was set up in 2016 for Sherlock Holmes enthusiasts to visit locations referred to in Sir Arthur Conan Doyle's books.
The tour also includes places where Sir Arthur visited and modern locations that celebrate one of the Literary world's most famous Detectives


The Sherlock Holmes museum is a treasure trove of memorabilia in Victorian style, to celebrate the great works of literature written by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle.

Inside the museum, Sherlock Holmes Study, 221B Baker Street Museum

The English Heritage Blue plaques commemorate notable locations and houses of historical interest.
